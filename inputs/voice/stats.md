## Indicative client results (frame as "what good looks like", not promises)

These are realistic Phase 1-3 KPIs from the keyword research roadmap.
Always frame as "what we work toward", not "what we guarantee."

- **By month 3:** 1-3 keywords in top 50, technical audit recommendations
  shipped, GBP fully optimised, baseline reporting live
- **By month 6:** 5+ keywords in top 30, 1-2 in top 10, 500+ monthly
  organic visits to client site (varies by starting point)
- **By month 12:** 15+ keywords in top 10, 2,000+ monthly organic visits,
  measurable lead lift attributable to organic (where tracking allows)

These numbers are starting-point sensitive. A brand-new domain with
zero backlinks takes longer. An existing site with 200 referring
domains moves faster. Always pre-qualify.

## Warnings / thresholds

Used to flag when a prospect isn't a good fit for SEO yet.

- **Site age:** if the domain is <6 months old, SEO is the wrong first
  move. Spend on Google Ads to test conversion, then SEO once you know
  what to target.
- **Conversion rate:** if the site converts <1% of organic traffic, fix
  the site before driving more traffic to it.
- **Budget floor:** under $1,800/mo total SEO budget, the work isn't
  meaningful. Say so honestly.
- **Service area:** if the prospect serves nationwide and has a $1.8K
  budget, that budget covers two suburbs of competitive intent. Pick.
