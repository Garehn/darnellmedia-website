# Project brief — Darnell Media

## Domain
darnellmedia.com

## What this site is
Sydney-based SEO agency for tradies and home-service businesses, with
first-mover positioning in AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) and GEO
(Generative Engine Optimisation). Founded 2025. The site sells discovery
calls and audits, not retainers directly — the retainer conversation
happens after the audit.

## Audience
Owner-operators of trade businesses (plumbers, electricians, pest
control, landscapers, vets, solar installers) in Sydney metro, plus
marketing managers at scaling home-service brands across Australia.
Most arrive on mobile. Most have been burned by a previous agency that
charged $3-5K/month for "SEO" and showed no rankings. Highly sceptical
of generic agency promises. Respect plain language and real numbers.

## What we're optimising for
1. **Lead generation** — discovery call bookings, form fills, calls
2. **Authority signal** for AEO/GEO (the category is uncontested in AU)
3. **Organic traffic** to the Phase 1 commercial keyword clusters

## Business model
Real agency taking clients. Funnel:
  Discovery call (free, 20 min) → SEO audit ($1,200 flat) → retainer.
No upsells, no "comprehensive digital marketing solutions", no contracts
longer than 6 months.

## Tone in one sentence
Confident, plain-spoken, occasionally dry, never corporate, never
patronising — like an SEO consultant explaining the work to a tradie
mate over coffee.

## Geographic focus
Primary: Sydney metro (Northern Beaches → Sydney CBD → Eastern Suburbs
→ Inner West). Secondary: Australia-wide for remote retainers and
informational keywords. National expansion (Melbourne / Brisbane /
Perth) is on the 24-month roadmap but not the current build.

## Hard rules — what to NEVER do
- No fake reviews, fake clients, fake testimonials, fake staff
- Never claim experience the founder doesn't have (Darnell Media
  launched in 2025 — own that, don't fake "10 years of experience")
- No "unlock", "leverage", "comprehensive", "world-class", "best-in-class",
  "seamless", "synergy", "empower", "cutting-edge"
- No "Whether you're..." sentence openers
- No "In today's fast-paced digital landscape" or any variant
- No exclamation marks anywhere
- No emojis in body copy
- No em dashes — use commas, full stops, or parentheses
- No three-item -ing lists ("ensuring, delivering, providing")
- No bullet points where every bullet is a full sentence ending with a period
- No closing sentences that restate the opening
- No fake star-rating widgets or trust badges we haven't earned

## Hard rules — what to ALWAYS do
- Australian English spelling (optimise, organisation, colour, behaviour)
- AUD pricing (with $ prefix, no decimals on round numbers — "$1,200" not "$1,200.00")
- Mobile-first composition (heroes legible on 412px)
- Cite real Australian regulators / bodies when claims need backing
  (ACMA, ATO, Fair Trading NSW, ABS)
- Tradie-friendly examples preferred over corporate SaaS examples
- Real numbers from `voice/stats.md` — never round, never invent
- Tell people when NOT to hire us at least once per commercial page
- One opinion per page, one story per page maximum
- Single H1 per page, sequential heading order
- Every commercial page ends with a contact CTA
